  • Yuji Itadori

    Main Protagonist

    Yuji Itadori is a high school student who becomes a vessel for Sukuna, the King of Curses, after eating one of his fingers. Despite his immense strength and agility, Yuji is kind-hearted and determined to protect others.

    Abilities

    Yuji possesses superhuman strength and speed, making him a formidable fighter. He also has the ability to host Sukuna, allowing him access to the curse's immense power.

    • Strength

      Superhuman

    • Agility

      High

    • Curse Energy

      Limited

    • Technique

      None

    • Potential

      Unlimited

  • Megumi Fushiguro

    Grade 2 Sorcerer

    Megumi Fushiguro is a stoic sorcerer who uses the Ten Shadows Technique to summon shikigami. Despite his serious demeanor, he is deeply caring and believes in saving those he deems worthy.

    Abilities

    Megumi can summon various shikigami, such as the Divine Dogs and Nue. His unique approach to sorcery allows him to manipulate shadows and enhance his combat capabilities.

    • Summoning

      Shikigami

    • Combat Skills

      High

    • Curse Energy

      Above Average

    • Technique

      Ten Shadows

    • Potential

      Great

  • Nobara Kugisaki

    Grade 3 Sorcerer

    Nobara Kugisaki is a fierce and confident sorcerer who uses her Straw Doll Technique to defeat curses. She values her individuality and isn't afraid to stand out.

    Abilities

    Nobara wields a hammer and nails to perform exorcisms using her Straw Doll Technique. Her tactical mind and fearlessness make her a valuable ally in battle.

  • Satoru Gojo

    Jujutsu Sorcerer

    Satoru Gojo is the most powerful Jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High. He possesses limitless curse energy and incredible techniques that make him nearly invincible in battle.

    Abilities

    Gojo's Limitless Cursed Technique allows him to manipulate space at an atomic level, making him an exceptionally formidable opponent.

    • Curse Energy

      Limitless

    • Strength

      Exceptional

    • Speed

      Blazing Fast

    • Durability

      Near Invincible

  • Maki Zenin

    Jujutsu Sorcerer

    Maki Zenin is a talented Jujutsu sorcerer from the Zenin family who lacks curse energy but makes up for it with her exceptional physical abilities and weapon expertise.

    Abilities

    Maki excels in weapon-based combat, particularly with cursed tools, and has incredible physical prowess despite her lack of curse energy.
